# EIKEN 4 (4級) - Reference Profile

**Source:** 2025 EIKEN Test Papers (1st, 2nd, 3rd Sessions)  
**Last Verified:** 2026-03-23  
**Status:** ✅ **Active Reference** (Use for all Eiken 4 content creation)

---

## 1. CEFR Alignment & Role
- **Level:** **A1 (Early)** / **Below A1** (Transition from Eiken 5)
- **Role:** The **"Narrative" Level**. Students move from describing the *now* (Eiken 5) to talking about **what happened** (Past) and **what will happen** (Future).

## 2. Carried Over from Eiken 5 (Review Only)
*These are assumed known. Do not focus on them as the primary learning goal.*
- **Grammar:** Present Simple, Present Continuous, Basic Modals (`can`, `let's`).
- **Vocab:** Daily routine, family, school, basic hobbies, weather, colors, numbers.
- **Sentence Length:** 4-6 words.

## 3. NEW Grammar Focus (The Core)
*These are the **only** new structures introduced at this level.*

| Grammar Point | Function | Example from 2025 Papers |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Past Simple** | **NEW:** Completed actions, past events | "I **went** to a party." <br> "It **was** very hot." <br> "He **played** well." <br> "Last week, a new student **came**." |
| **Future (will / going to)** | **NEW:** Plans, predictions | "The festival **will be** open." <br> "I **will bring** some cakes." <br> "We **are going to** a party." |
| **Past Questions** | Asking about the past | "**Did** you **like** the book?" <br> "**What did** you **wear**?" <br> "**How was** your trip?" |
| **Future Questions** | Asking about plans | "**When will** you **come**?" <br> "**What will** you **bring**?" |

### ❌ **Strictly Excluded (Do Not Use)**
- **Present Perfect** (have/has + past participle) - *Zero instances in 2025 tests.*
- **Past Continuous** (was/were + -ing) - *Rare to non-existent.*
- **Conditionals** (If... then...) - *Not tested.*
- **Comparatives/Superlatives** (bigger, best) - *Very rare, avoid.*
- **Complex Conjunctions** (although, unless) - *Only basic `and`, `but`, `so`.*

## 4. Vocabulary Characterization
*Focus on **narrative** and **planning** themes.*
- **Time Markers:** `last week`, `yesterday`, `ago`, `last night`, `tomorrow`, `next week`, `soon`.
- **Past Events:** `party`, `trip`, `camping`, `contest`, `festival`, `visit`, `game`, `movie`.
- **Future Plans:** `plan`, `hope`, `want`, `need`, `bring`, `meet`, `go`, `come`.
- **Feelings (Past/Future):** `happy`, `sad`, `tired`, `excited`, `wonderful`, `great`, `difficult`, `easy`.
- **Verbs (Past):** `went`, `came`, `saw`, `ate`, `took`, `got`, `made`, `said`, `had`, `did`, `played`, `watched`, `studied`, `helped`, `called`, `asked`, `answered`, `opened`, `closed`, `started`, `stopped`, `finished`, `bought`, `read`, `wrote`, `ran`, `walked`, `swam`, `flew`, `drove`, `slept`, `drank`.
- **Verbs (Future):** `will`, `going to`, `plan to`, `want to`, `hope to`, `need to`, `have to`.

## 5. Sentence Structure & Length
- **Length:** **5-8 words** average. (Slightly longer than Eiken 5).
- **Structure:** Simple SVO, but **compound sentences** with `and`, `but`, `so` are now allowed.
  - *Example:* "I played soccer **and** had fun."
  - *Example:* "It was hot, **but** we swam."
- **Complexity:** Can combine two ideas with `and`, `but`, `so`. No `because`, `if` yet.

## 7. Allowed Patterns Only (Strict Positive Constraints)
*The AI must ONLY use these patterns. If a pattern is not listed below, it is FORBIDDEN.*

### ✅ **ALLOWED Verb Forms**
- **Present Simple:** All base verbs.
- **Past Simple:** All common irregulars (went, came, saw, ate, took, got, made, said, had, did, played, watched, studied, helped, called, asked, answered, opened, closed, started, stopped, finished, bought, read, wrote, ran, walked, swam, flew, drove, slept, drank).
- **Future:** `will` + base verb, `going to` + base verb.
- **Continuous:** `am/is/are + [verb]-ing` (for "now" or "future arrangement").
- **Modals:** `can`, `could`, `should`, `may`, `might`.

### ✅ **ALLOWED Question Words**
- `What`, `Where`, `Who`, `When`, `How`, `Did`, `Was`, `Were`, `Will`, `Can`, `Could`, `Should`.

### ✅ **ALLOWED Sentence Structures**
- **Simple SVO:** "I played soccer."
- **Compound SVO:** "I played soccer **and** had fun." (Use `and`, `but`, `so`)
- **Past Narrative:** "Last week, I went to the park."
- **Future Plan:** "I will bring cakes." / "We are going to a party."

### ❌ **FORBIDDEN (If seen, the output is INVALID)**
- **Present Perfect:** `have`, `has`, `had` + past participle.
- **Past Continuous:** `was/were + -ing`.
- **Conditionals:** `if`, `unless`.
- **Comparatives/Superlatives:** `bigger`, `best`, `more`, `most`.
- **Complex Links:** `because`, `although`, `whereas`.

## 8. AI Validation Checklist (Run Before Output)
*If ANY item is unchecked, the content is INVALID.*

- [ ] **Verb Check:** Does every verb match the "ALLOWED Verb Forms" list (Present, Past Simple, Future will/going to)?
- [ ] **Tense Check:** Is there **NO** Present Perfect (`have/has + ed`)?
- [ ] **Question Check:** Are all questions using `What`, `Where`, `Who`, `When`, `How`, `Did`, `Was`, `Were`, `Will`?
- [ ] **Length Check:** Is every sentence **≤ 8 words**?
- [ ] **Linking Check:** Are compound sentences using only `and`, `but`, `so` (no `because`, `if`)?
- [ ] **Topic Check:** Is the topic concrete (past events, future plans, school, travel)?
