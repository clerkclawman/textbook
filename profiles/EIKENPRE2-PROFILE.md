# EIKEN Pre-2 (準2級) - Reference Profile

**Source:** 2025 EIKEN Test Papers (1st, 2nd, 3rd Sessions)  
**Last Verified:** 2026-03-23  
**Status:** ✅ **Active Reference** (Use for all Eiken Pre-2 content creation)

---

## 1. CEFR Alignment & Role
- **Level:** **A2 (Mid-High)**
- **Role:** The **"Opinion & Argument" Level**. Students express **opinions**, discuss **abstract topics** (history, culture, environment), and handle **complex narratives** (past continuous, reported speech, passive voice).

## 2. Carried Over from Eiken 3 (Review Only)
*These are assumed known. Do not focus on them as the primary learning goal.*
- **Grammar:** Present Perfect, Past Simple, First Conditional, Superlatives, Basic Passive, Reported Speech (intro).
- **Vocab:** Experiences, opinions, comparisons, abstract topics (culture, history, travel).
- **Sentence Length:** 6-10 words.

## 3. NEW Grammar Focus (The Core)
*These are the **only** new structures introduced at this level.*

| Grammar Point | Function | Example from 2025 Papers |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Past Continuous** | **NEW:** Ongoing past actions, background | "Some people **were enjoying** bird watching." <br> "While I **was walking** around, I saw..." <br> "Five years later, Sophie **was watching** a movie." |
| **Passive Voice (Advanced)** | **NEW:** Present perfect passive, future passive | "The ways **have been limited** for a long time." <br> "It **will be controlled** properly." <br> "The spots **have been filled**." |
| **Reported Speech (Backshift)** | **NEW:** Tense shift in reported clauses | "He said that he **wanted** to be an actor." <br> "He told her that he **had been** there." <br> "The report said that the storm **would** come." |
| **Complex Conjunctions** | **NEW:** `while`, `although`, `since`, `as` | "**While** I was walking, I saw..." <br> "**Although** it was hot, we swam." <br> "**Since** the road was closed, we took a bus." |
| **Modals of Advice/Obligation** | **NEW:** `should`, `must`, `have to`, `need to` (stronger) | "She **should care** more." <br> "You **must finish** this." <br> "We **need to print** something." |

### ❌ **Strictly Excluded (Do Not Use)**
- **Past Perfect** (had + past participle) - *Rare, avoid unless necessary for sequence.*
- **Second/Third Conditionals** (If I were..., If I had known...) - *Only Type 1, maybe light Type 2.*
- **Present Perfect Continuous** (have been + -ing) - *Rare to non-existent.*
- **Complex Relative Clauses** (who, which, that with multiple clauses) - *Keep simple.*
- **Subjunctive Mood** (If I were, I suggest that he be) - *Not tested.*

## 5. Sentence Structure & Length
- **Length:** **10-16 words** average. (Some up to 18-20 for complex ideas).
- **Structure:** Complex sentences with `while`, `although`, `because`, `if`, `when`, `since`, `as`, `and`, `but`, `so`.
  - *Example:* "**While** I **was walking** around, I **saw** a strange bird." (11 words)
  - *Example:* "The ways **have been limited** for a long time." (8 words)
  - *Example:* "He said that he **wanted** to be an actor." (9 words)
- **Complexity:** Can combine multiple clauses with `while`, `although`, `because`, `if`, `when`, `since`, `as`.

## 6. Example Sentences (Direct from 2025 Tests)
*These are the exact patterns to emulate.*

**Past Continuous (Ongoing Actions):**
> "Some people **were enjoying** bird watching."
> "While I **was walking** around, I saw..."
> "Five years later, Sophie **was watching** a Spanish movie."
> "It **was getting** dark, so we went home."
> "They **were discussing** the problem when I arrived."

**Passive Voice (Advanced):**
> "The ways **have been limited** for a long time."
> "It **will be controlled** properly."
> "The spots **have been filled**."
> "The village **is known** as the only one..."
> "Roads **were asked** if they wanted to build..."

**Reported Speech (Backshift):**
> "He said that he **wanted** to be an actor."
> "He told her that he **had been** there."
> "The report said that the storm **would** come."
> "She **explained that** she **was** busy."
> "They **mentioned that** they **had finished** the work."

**Complex Conjunctions:**
> "**While** I was walking around, I saw..."
> "**Although** it was hot, we swam."
> "**Since** the road was closed, we took a bus."
> "**As** the storm was coming, we stayed inside."
> "**Because** the road was blocked, we turned back."

**Modals of Advice/Obligation:**
> "She **should care** more about her friends."
> "You **must finish** this by tomorrow."
> "We **need to print** something."
> "They **have to study** harder."
> "He **ought to apologize**."

**Questions (Wh- & Yes/No):**
> "How was Todd able to contact Ms. Parker?"
> "What is one thing Todd wants to ask...?"
> "Which is correct to describe Chamois?"
> "Why did the people of Chamois want cable cars?"
> "What is true about Chamois today?"
> "How does salt help people's lives today?"

## 7. Allowed Patterns Only (Strict Positive Constraints)
*The AI must ONLY use these patterns. If a pattern is not listed below, it is FORBIDDEN.*

### ✅ **ALLOWED Verb Forms**
- **Present Simple:** All base verbs.
- **Past Simple:** All common irregulars.
- **Past Continuous:** `was/were + [verb]-ing`.
- **Present Perfect:** `have/has + past participle`.
- **Future:** `will`, `going to`, `can`, `could`, `may`, `might`, `would`, `should`, `must`, `have to`, `need to`.
- **Conditional (Type 1):** `If + Present Simple, will/can/may + base verb`.
- **Comparatives/Superlatives:** `more`, `less`, `better`, `worse`, `best`, `worst`, `biggest`, `smallest`, `most`, `least`, `-er`, `-est`.
- **Passive (All Tenses):** `am/is/are/was/were/has been/have been/will be + past participle`.
- **Reported Speech (Backshift):** `said that`, `told me`, `asked if`, `explained that`, `mentioned that`, `wondered`, `wanted to know`.

### ✅ **ALLOWED Question Words**
- `What`, `Where`, `Who`, `When`, `Why`, `How`, `How long`, `How many`, `How often`, `Which`, `Did`, `Was`, `Were`, `Will`, `Can`, `Could`, `Have`, `Has`, `Do`, `Does`, `Should`, `Must`, `Have to`, `Need to`.

### ✅ **ALLOWED Sentence Structures**
- **Simple SVO:** "I have been to Japan."
- **Complex (While/Although/Because):** "While I was walking, I saw..."
- **Comparative:** "This is more interesting than that."
- **Passive:** "The road was closed." / "The ways have been limited."
- **Reported:** "She said that he wanted to be an actor."
- **Compound:** "I went to the park, and I played soccer."

### ❌ **FORBIDDEN (If seen, the output is INVALID)**
- **Past Perfect:** `had + past participle` (unless necessary for sequence, e.g., "After he had finished...").
- **Second/Third Conditionals:** `If I were...`, `If I had known...`.
- **Present Perfect Continuous:** `have been + -ing`.
- **Complex Modals:** `should have`, `could have`, `would have`, `must have`.
- **Complex Relative Clauses:** `who`, `which`, `that` with multiple clauses.
- **Subjunctive Mood:** `If I were`, `I suggest that he be`.

## 8. AI Validation Checklist (Run Before Output)
*If ANY item is unchecked, the content is INVALID.*

- [ ] **Verb Check:** Does every verb match the "ALLOWED Verb Forms" list (Present, Past, Past Continuous, Present Perfect, Future, Passive, Reported)?
- [ ] **Tense Check:** Is there **NO** Past Perfect (`had + ed`) unless necessary for sequence?
- [ ] **Conditional Check:** Are all conditionals **Type 1** only (`If + Present, will/can`)?
- [ ] **Question Check:** Are all questions using `What`, `Where`, `Who`, `When`, `Why`, `How`, `Have`, `Has`, `Did`, `Will`, `Can`, `Should`, `Must`?
- [ ] **Length Check:** Is every sentence **≤ 18 words** (average 12-14)?
- [ ] **Complexity Check:** Are compound/complex sentences using `while`, `although`, `because`, `if`, `when`, `since`, `as`, `and`, `but`, `so`?
- [ ] **Topic Check:** Is the topic abstract (history, culture, environment, opinion, argument)?
