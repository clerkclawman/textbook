## Eiken 3 Specifications

### Target Audience
- Junior high school graduates (middle school level)
- Bridge to high school English

### Sentence Length: 7-9 words
Step up from Eiken 4's 6-7 words

### Grammar Focus
Eiken 3 introduces:
1. **Present Perfect** - "I have been to Tokyo."
2. **Passive Voice** - "The book was written by..."
3. **Conditionals (Type 1)** - "If it rains, I will stay home."
4. **Reported Speech** - "He said that..."
5. **Modal Verbs** - should, must, might, could
