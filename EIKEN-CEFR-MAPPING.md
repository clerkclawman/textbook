## 2. Typical Sentence Length and Structure

| Grade | Avg. Words | Shortest Example | Longest Example | Patterns |
|-------|------------|------------------|-----------------|----------|
| 5 | 6 | "I'm Tom." | "My brother and I come to school by bus every day." | SVO, Present Progressive |
| 3 | 12 | "Was it hot?" | "Research by scientists shows that global warming is a big problem." | Present Perfect, Passives |
| 1 | 22+ | "He is lucky." | "As well as being a successful businessman, Mr. Gibbons is known for his ( )." | Complex clauses, Nominalization |

### Common Patterns

**Grade 5-4:**
- Direct commands ("Please open")
- Simple description ("It is very heavy")

**Grade 3-2:**
- Comparative structures ("cooler than my hometown")
- Subordinate clauses ("Although it may seem...")

**Grade Pre-1/1:**
- Introductory participle phrases ("Following his outburst...")
- Conditional counter-factuals ("Without such a system...")

## 3. Types of Questions

- **Yes/No Questions:** Primarily Grade 5–3
  - Example: "Do you play basketball, Bob?"

- **Wh- Questions:** All levels
  - Example: "What language does Bryan study?"

- **Opinion Questions:** Grade 3–1 (Writing Tasks)
  - Example: "Do you think it is good for children to decide what time they go to bed...?"

- **Comprehension Questions:** Grade 4–1 (Reading/Listening)
  - Example: "What is Jessica interested in about the library?"

---

## 4. Required Communication Skills

- **Asking for Information:** "Is there a subway station near here?" (G4)
- **Describing Experiences:** "I went to a party this weekend." (G4)
- **Giving Opinions:** "Do you think more people will enjoy this activity in the future?" (Pre-2)
- **Synthesizing Information:** Summarising technical texts in "your own words" (Pre-2 Plus to G1)

## 6. Content Generation Specification (AI Instructions)

### General Rules
- **Tone:** Neutral, informative, and polite
- **Format:** Always multiple-choice (4 options) for reading/listening

### Level-Specific Constraints

#### Grade 5 (A1)
- **Grammar:** Present Simple/Continuous ONLY
- **Length:** Max 10 words/sentence
- **Topics:** Home, School, Habits
- **Banned:** All past tenses, relative clauses

#### Grade 3 (A2)
- **Grammar:** Present Perfect, Passives, Comparatives
- **Length:** Max 15 words/sentence
- **Topics:** Basic science, simple news
- **Banned:** Hypothetical conditionals (e.g., "If I were you...")

#### Grade 1 (C1)
- **Grammar:** Advanced nominalization, counter-factuals
- **Length:** 20–35 words/sentence
- **Topics:** Geopolitics, high-level science, business law
- **Banned:** Basic daily greetings or overly simple SVO structures
