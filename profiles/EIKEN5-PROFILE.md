# EIKEN 5 (5級) - Reference Profile

**Source:** 2025 EIKEN Test Papers (1st, 2nd, 3rd Sessions)  
**Last Verified:** 2026-03-23  
**Status:** ✅ **Active Reference** (Use for all Eiken 5 content creation)

---

## 1. CEFR Alignment
- **Level:** **Below A1** (Pre-A1)
- **Rationale:** Exclusively **Present Simple** and **Present Continuous**. No Past, Future, or Perfect tenses (based on 2025 test data).
- **Role:** The "Survival" level. Focus on immediate context, identity, and daily routines.

## 2. Target Audience
- **Age:** Elementary School (Grades 3-5, approx. 8-11 years old)
- **Context:** School, family, hobbies, weather, basic needs.

## 3. Grammar Focus (The "Big 3")
*Only these structures appear in the 2025 tests.*

| Grammar Point | Function | Example from 2025 Papers |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Present Simple (Be-Verb)** | Identity, Location, Description | "He **is** in the garden." <br> "Where **is** Ken?" <br> "She **is** very cute." |
| **Present Simple (Action)** | Habits, Routines, Facts | "I **play** soccer." <br> "Do you **have** any sisters?" <br> "I **like** tennis." |
| **Present Continuous** | Actions happening *now* | "What **is** Mike **doing** now?" <br> "He **is reading**." |
| **Modals (Can)** | Ability / Permission | "**Can** you help me?" <br> "**Can** I go?" |
| **Imperatives / Suggestions** | Basic requests | "**Let's** play badminton." |

### ❌ **Strictly Excluded (Do Not Use)**
- **Past Simple** (went, played, was, were)
- **Future Forms** (will, going to)
- **Present Perfect** (have done, has been)
- **Comparatives/Superlatives** (bigger, best, more)
- **Complex Conjunctions** (because, although, if, when [as a connector])
- **Complex Wh- Questions** (Why, How often, What time - *only What, Where, Who, When [date]* appear)
- **Type 2/3 Conditionals**

## 4. Vocabulary Description
- **Scope:** ~300-400 core words.
- **Categories:**
  - **People:** Family (mom, dad, sister, brother), Teachers, Friends.
  - **School:** Classroom, teacher, lesson, computer, book, pencil, test.
  - **Daily Life:** Home, room, bed, food (breakfast, lunch, dinner), clothes (T-shirt, pajamas, shoes).
  - **Hobbies:** Soccer, basketball, tennis, swimming, music, art, reading.
  - **Nature:** Weather (sunny, cloudy, cold, hot, rain), animals (dog, cat).
  - **Time:** Today, tomorrow, now, weekend, morning, afternoon, evening, date (April 29).
  - **Adjectives:** Very basic (nice, cute, short, long, big, small, hot, cold, happy, sad).
  - **Verbs:** High-frequency action verbs (play, go, come, have, like, do, see, listen, watch, help, eat, drink, sleep, read, write).

## 5. Sentence Structure & Length
- **Length:** **4-6 words** maximum. (Rarely 7).
- **Structure:** Simple Subject-Verb-Object (SVO).
  - *Example:* "I play soccer on weekends." (6 words)
  - *Example:* "Where is Ken?" (3 words)
- **Complexity:** **No compound sentences.** Avoid linking clauses with "and", "but", "so".
  - *Bad:* "I like soccer and I play it."
  - *Good:* "I like soccer. I play it." (Two sentences)

## 6. Example Sentences (Direct from 2025 Tests)
*These are the exact patterns to emulate.*

**Identity & Location (Be-Verb):**
> "He **is** in the garden."
> "Where **is** Ken?"
> "She **is** very cute."
> "Mr. Brown **is** a music teacher."

**Habits & Routines (Present Simple):**
> "I **play** soccer."
> "Do you **have** any sisters?"
> "I **like** tennis."
> "I **go** to school."
> "I **often watch** basketball games."

**Current Actions (Present Continuous):**
> "What **is** Mike **doing** now?"
> "He **is reading**."
> "What **is** Scott **doing** now?"

**Ability & Permission (Can):**
> "**Can** you help me?"
> "**Can** you come to my house today?"
> "I **can't** play soccer today."

**Suggestions:**
> "**Let's** play badminton at the park."
> "**Let's** start today's lesson."

**Questions (Wh-):**
> "What's your favorite color?"
> "Where are my pajamas?"
> "When do you study English?"
> "What's the date today?"

## 7. Allowed Patterns Only (Strict Positive Constraints)
*The AI must ONLY use these patterns. If a pattern is not listed below, it is FORBIDDEN.*

### ✅ **ALLOWED Verb Forms**
- **Be-Verb:** `am`, `is`, `are` (Present Simple)
- **Action Verbs:** `play`, `go`, `come`, `have`, `like`, `do`, `see`, `listen`, `watch`, `help`, `eat`, `drink`, `sleep`, `read`, `write`, `study`, `talk`, `walk`, `run`, `sit`, `stand`, `open`, `close`, `take`, `give`, `make`, `get`, `put`, `say`, `tell`, `ask`, `answer`, `know`, `think`, `want`, `need`, `look`, `find`, `use`, `work`, `live`, `love`, `hate`, `feel`, `become`, `leave`, `start`, `stop`, `finish`, `begin`, `end`, `try`, `help`, `let`, `make`, `have`, `can`, `let's`
- **Continuous:** `is/are + [verb]-ing` (only for "now" context)
- **Modal:** `can`, `can't` (only for ability/permission)

### ✅ **ALLOWED Question Words**
- `What`, `Where`, `Who`, `When` (only for date/day), `Yes/No` (Do/Does/Is/Are/Can)

### ✅ **ALLOWED Sentence Structures**
- **Simple SVO:** `Subject + Verb + Object` (e.g., "I play soccer.")
- **Simple Be:** `Subject + am/is/are + Noun/Adj` (e.g., "He is happy.")
- **Continuous:** `Subject + am/is/are + Verb-ing` (e.g., "She is running.")
- **Question:** `Wh-word + Aux + Subject + Verb?` (e.g., "What is he doing?")

### ❌ **FORBIDDEN (If seen, the output is INVALID)**
- **Any Past Tense:** `was`, `were`, `went`, `played`, `had`, `did`, `saw`, `ate`, `took`, `got`, `made`, `said`, `knew`, `thought`, `came`, `left`, `felt`, `became`, `found`, `gave`, `put`, `set`, `let`, `met`, `kept`, `built`, `bought`, `taught`, `caught`, `brought`, `heard`, `understood`, `spent`, `sent`
- **Any Future:** `will`, `going to`, `shall`
- **Any Perfect:** `have`, `has`, `had` + past participle
- **Comparatives/Superlatives:** `bigger`, `best`, `more`, `most`, `taller`, `faster`
- **Complex Links:** `because`, `although`, `if`, `when` (as connector), `so`, `but`
- **Abstract Nouns:** `dream`, `plan`, `future`, `hope`, `wish`, `idea`, `opinion`, `feeling` (beyond happy/sad)

## 8. AI Validation Checklist (Run Before Output)
*If ANY item is unchecked, the content is INVALID.*

- [ ] **Verb Check:** Does every verb match the "ALLOWED Verb Forms" list (Present Simple, Continuous)?
- [ ] **Question Check:** Are all questions using only `What`, `Where`, `Who`, `When` (date), or Yes/No?
- [ ] **Length Check:** Is every sentence **≤ 6 words**?
- [ ] **Tense Check:** Is there **NO** Past, Future, or Perfect tense?
- [ ] **Linking Check:** Are there **NO** `and`, `but`, `because` joining clauses?
