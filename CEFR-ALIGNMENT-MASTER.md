## 2025 Test Paper Evidence

### Eiken 3 (Grade 3) - A2 Confirmation
**Grammar Found in 2025 Tests:**
- ✅ **Present Perfect:** "Have you been to the new zoo?", "I haven't seen Karen for a while"
- ✅ **Past Simple (extensive):** "How long did Ms. Wilson work...", "Why did Lucas write..."
- ✅ **Future Forms:** "When will Lucas's grandfather be able to meet...", "I'm going to Hawaii"
- ✅ **First Conditionals:** "If it's not in the morning, I can meet you"
- ✅ **"Be able to":** "When will... be able to meet..."

**Sentence Complexity:** 10-15 words, multiple clauses

**Conclusion:** This is **clearly A2**, not A1. A1 would only have present simple and basic questions.

## Progression Logic

### Below A1 (Eiken 5-6-7)
- Present simple only
- Basic vocabulary (family, school, daily routine)
- 4-5 word sentences
- No past/future complexity

### A1 (Eiken 4)
- Introduction to past simple
- Basic "will" for future
- Simple questions (What/Where/Who)
- 5-6 word sentences

### **A2 (Eiken 3 → Pre-2 → Pre-2 Plus)**
- **Early A2 (Eiken 3):** Present perfect, past simple, first conditionals
- **Mid A2 (Pre-2):** Past continuous, more complex conditionals
- **Late A2 (Pre-2 Plus):** Passive voice intro, reported speech intro
- 6-10 word sentences
- Abstract concepts begin

### B1 (Eiken 2)
- Full passive voice
- Reported speech
- Type 2 conditionals
- Opinion expression
- 10-15 word sentences

### B2+ (Pre-1, Eiken 1)
- Complex grammar structures
- Academic/professional vocabulary
- Nuanced argumentation
- 15+ word sentences

## Content Creation Guidelines

### When Creating Stories for Each Level

**Eiken 5 (Below A1):**
- ✅ Present simple only
- ✅ 4-5 word sentences
- ✅ Concrete topics (family, school, hobbies)
- ❌ No past tense, no conditionals

**Eiken 4 (A1 early):**
- ✅ Present simple + basic past simple
- ✅ Basic "will" for future
- ✅ 5-6 word sentences
- ❌ No present perfect, no complex conditionals

**Eiken 3 (A2 early):**
- ✅ Present perfect (core focus)
- ✅ Past simple (extensive)
- ✅ First conditionals
- ✅ Superlatives/comparatives
- ✅ 6-8 word sentences
- ❌ No passive voice, no reported speech (yet)

**Pre-2 (A2 mid-high):**
- ✅ Past continuous
- ✅ More complex conditionals
- ✅ Future forms (going to, will, present continuous)
- ✅ 7-9 word sentences

**Pre-2 Plus (A2/B1 bridge):**
- ✅ Passive voice (basic)
- ✅ Reported speech (intro)
- ✅ Type 2 conditionals (light exposure)
- ✅ 8-10 word sentences

**Eiken 2 (B1):**
- ✅ Full passive voice
- ✅ Reported speech (mastery)
- ✅ Type 2 conditionals
- ✅ Opinion expressions
- ✅ 10-12 word sentences

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

1. **Never guess CEFR alignment** - Always verify with actual test papers
2. **Don't assume Pre-2 Plus is B1** - It's A2/B1 bridge
3. **Don't put A2 grammar in Eiken 4** - Keep it below A1/A1 early
4. **Don't make Eiken 3 too easy** - It needs present perfect and conditionals
5. **Don't skip the progression** - Each level must build smoothly on the previous
