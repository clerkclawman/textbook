## 📊 Step-by-Step Level Progression

### 1. Eiken 7 (Theoretical) - The "Zero" Point
* **Target:** Grades 3-4 (8-10 yrs)
* **Core Concept:** **Structure & Sound Awareness.**
* **Grammar:** Only **Present Simple** (Be-verbs + basic actions). No tenses.
* **Key Difference:** **SVO Order** (I eat) vs. Japanese SOV. **Pronouns are mandatory** (No dropping "I").
* **Vocab:** Concrete, immediate (Family, Colors, Animals, Body).
* **Sentence Length:** 3-5 words.
* **Critical Check:** Does the sentence have a subject? Is the word order SVO? Are final consonants clear?
* **[📄 Read Full Profile](./profiles/EIKEN7-PROFILE.md)**

### 2. Eiken 6 (Theoretical) - The "Aspect" Bridge
* **Target:** Grades 5-6 (10-12 yrs)
* **Core Concept:** **Introducing Aspect (Continuous) & Grammar Mechanics.**
* **Grammar:** **Present Continuous** (is eating), **Plurals** (cats), **Articles** (a/an). (**NO Past/Future yet**).
* **Key Difference:** Distinguishing "Now" (-ing) vs. "Always" (Simple). Marking **Plurals** (-s) and **Articles** (a/an).
* **Vocab:** Daily routines, hobbies, weather, time markers (now, today).
* **Sentence Length:** 4-7 words.
* **Critical Check:** Is the verb form correct for "now" (-ing)? Are plurals marked? Are articles used correctly?
* **[📄 Read Full Profile](./profiles/EIKEN6-PROFILE.md)**

### 3. Eiken 5 - The "Below A1" Foundation
* **Target:** Junior High Year 1 (12-13 yrs)
* **Core Concept:** **Routine & Description (Present Tense Only).**
* **Grammar:** Mastery of **Present Simple** & **Present Continuous**. Basic "Can". (**NO Past/Future yet**).
* **Key Difference:** No past/future yet. Focus on **habits** and **current actions**.
* **Vocab:** School, family, daily life, basic feelings.
* **Sentence Length:** 4-6 words.
* **Critical Check:** Are all verbs in Present Simple or Continuous? No past tense allowed.
* **[📄 Read Full Profile](./profiles/EIKEN5-PROFILE.md)**

### 4. Eiken 4 - The "Narrative" Step
* **Target:** Junior High Year 2 (13-14 yrs)
* **Core Concept:** **Telling Stories (Past & Future).**
* **Grammar:** **Past Simple** (regular/irregular), **Future** (will/going to). (**First level to introduce these**).
* **Key Difference:** Moving from "now" to "then" and "later".
* **Vocab:** Past events (trips, parties), future plans, basic feelings.
* **Sentence Length:** 5-8 words.
* **Critical Check:** Are past tense verbs used correctly? Is "will" used for future predictions/decisions?
* **[📄 Read Full Profile](./profiles/EIKEN4-PROFILE.md)**

### 5. Eiken 3 - The "Experience" Level (A2 Early)
* **Target:** Junior High Year 3 (14-15 yrs)
* **Core Concept:** **Experiences & Possibilities.**
* **Grammar:** **Present Perfect** (have been), **First Conditional** (If... will), **Superlatives**.
* **Key Difference:** Connecting past to present ("I have been"). Real possibilities ("If it rains").
* **Vocab:** Experiences, travel, opinions, comparisons.
* **Sentence Length:** 6-10 words.
* **Critical Check:** Is "have/has" used correctly for experiences? Are conditionals Type 1 only?
* **[📄 Read Full Profile](./profiles/EIKEN3-PROFILE.md)**

### 6. Eiken Pre-2 - The "Opinion" Level (A2 Mid-High)

* **Grammar:** **Past Continuous**, **Passive Voice** (basic), **Reported Speech** (intro).
* **Key Difference:** Describing background actions ("was walking") and shifting focus to the action ("was built").
* **Vocab:** Abstract topics (culture, history), opinions, complex feelings.
* **Sentence Length:** 10-16 words.

## 🛡️ Universal Validation Rules (All Levels)
1.  **Pronouns:** Never drop the subject (I, you, he, she, it, we, they).
2.  **Word Order:** Strict **SVO** (Subject-Verb-Object).
3.  **Final Sounds:** Ensure final consonants (t, d, s, k, p) are implied/present.
4.  **Complexity:** Do not exceed the sentence length limit for the level.
5.  **Grammar:** Do not introduce grammar points from higher levels (e.g., no Past Perfect in Eiken 4).
6.  **Progression Rule:** A **lower** level (e.g., Eiken 6) **MUST NOT** include grammar that a **higher** level (e.g., Eiken 5) forbids.
