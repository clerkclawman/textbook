# High School Question Content Ideas

## Existing Formats (Already Created)
- Have You Ever...
- Would You Rather...
- What's the Best / What's the Worst
- Getting to Know You
- Easy Questions

## Potential Formats to Create

### Hypothetical/Conditional
- What would you do if... (hypothetical scenarios)
- If you could... (wishes/abilities)
- What's the first thing you'd do if... (immediate reactions)
- What would happen if... (cause and effect)

### Comparisons/Choices
- Do you prefer... or... (comparisons)
- This or That (quick choices)
- What's the difference between... (comparisons)

### Opinions/Feelings
- What do you think about... (opinions)
- How do you feel about... (emotional responses)
- What's your opinion on... (controversial topics)

### Personal Preferences
- What's your favorite... (personal preferences)
- What's something you've always wanted to... (desires)
- What's the most... (superlatives - embarrassing, exciting, etc.)

### Abilities/Skills
- Can you... (abilities/skills)
- How do you handle... (problem-solving)

### Habits/Frequency
- How often do you... (habits/frequency)
- When was the last time you... (recent experiences)

### Explanations/Reasons
- Why do you... (reasons/explanations)
- What makes a good... (criteria/standards)

### Open-ended
- Tell me about... (open-ended descriptions)
- What advice would you give... (wisdom/experience)

## Notes
- Keep questions age-appropriate for high schoolers
- Avoid topics like love hotels, gambling, etc.
- Aim for mixed yes/no answers to spark discussion
- 50 questions per file for 50-minute lessons
